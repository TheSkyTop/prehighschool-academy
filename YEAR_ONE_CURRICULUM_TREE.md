# Year-One Curriculum Tree

## Design Principle

The year-one curriculum uses a spiral structure:

- Weeks 1-13: Foundation and core fluency.
- Weeks 14-26: Selective and scholarship skill building.
- Weeks 27-39: Cross-topic transfer and higher difficulty.
- Weeks 40-52: Exam simulation, review, and mastery.

Each subject has 52 topics. Every topic is one focused daily mission.
