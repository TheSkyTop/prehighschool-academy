# PreHighSchool Academy - Content Blueprint

## Content Strategy

The app uses one coherent learning path rather than separate country-specific courses. Australia is the core pathway. US and UK public curriculum structures enrich the scope and help raise the ceiling for selective, scholarship, and high-achievement preparation.

## Alignment Layers

Each lesson and question can be tagged with one or more layers:

- AUS_CORE: Australian Curriculum v9 and Australian classroom readiness.
- AUS_SELECTIVE: ACER, HAST, NAPLAN extension, scholarship-style preparation.
- US_COMMON_CORE: Common Core style mathematics and English language arts.
- US_NGSS: NGSS style science practices, disciplinary ideas, modelling, and data interpretation.
- UK_KS2: England Key Stage 2 English, mathematics, and science.
- UK_11_PLUS: 11+ style verbal, non-verbal, reading, and quantitative reasoning.
- GLOBAL_CHALLENGE: advanced primary-to-middle-school bridge content.

## Difficulty Scale

- Level 1: Year 4 Core
- Level 2: Year 5 Core
- Level 3: Year 6 Standard
- Level 4: Extension
- Level 5: Selective / Scholarship
- Level 6: Global Challenge

V1 should mainly use Levels 2 to 5. Level 6 should appear as optional challenge content.

## Test Types

### Daily Quest Quiz

Purpose: Build daily habit and reinforce one learning objective.

Typical size: 10-15 questions.

### Topic Mastery Quiz

Purpose: Decide whether the student has mastered a topic.

Typical size: 15-25 questions.

### Speed Challenge

Purpose: Improve pacing, estimation, skimming, and selective guessing.

Typical size: 8-20 questions with strict time limits.

### Selective Mixed Test

Purpose: Blend mathematics, reading, verbal reasoning, and quantitative reasoning.

Typical size: 30-60 questions.

### Scholarship Mock Test

Purpose: Higher difficulty practice including long reading, writing, science reasoning, and multi-step mathematics.

Typical size: 45-90 minutes split into sections.

- Mathematics: 350 questions

- English Language: 220 questions
- Writing: 80 prompts and 40 model plans
- Science: 250 questions
- Physics & Applied Science: 150 questions

Initial seed distribution:

- At least 1 topic and 2-3 questions per subject.
- At least one daily quest using mixed subjects.
- At least one parent-visible weak area and reward rule.
